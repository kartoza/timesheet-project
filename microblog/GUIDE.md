# Updates User Guide

A quick-start guide for using the Updates feature — posting updates, managing pins, and more.

---

## Overview

*(screenshot: Updates feed overview)*

## Creating a Post

1. Click the **+** button in the top-right corner of the Updates feed.
2. Fill in the **Content** field (required). Maximum 666 characters — a live counter is shown below the field.
3. Optionally configure the remaining fields (see sections below).
4. Click **Post** to publish.

*(screenshot: New Post modal)*

---

## Post Types

Use the **Type** dropdown to categorise your post. The type affects the visual style of the card.

| Type | Use for |
|---|---|
| Default | General updates |
| Announcement | Important notices |
| Tips | Helpful hints or best practices |
| Question | Asking the team something |
| Warning | Flagging an issue or risk |
| Celebration | Sharing wins or milestones |

*(screenshot: Type dropdown options)*

## Tags

Tags help organise and search posts.

- Type a tag name in the **Add tag** field and press **Enter** or **,** to add it.
- Click the **+** button as an alternative to pressing Enter.
- Tags are stored without the `#` prefix — it is added automatically when displayed.
- Click the **×** on a tag chip to remove it before posting.

*(screenshot: Tags input with example chips)*

## Visibility Range

You can schedule when a post is visible using the **Visible from** and **Visible until** date/time fields.

- **Visible from** — the post will not appear in the feed before this date/time.
- **Visible until** — the post will disappear from the feed after this date/time.
- Leave both empty for the post to be visible immediately and indefinitely.

Dates are entered and displayed in **DD/MM/YYYY HH:mm** format.

## Pinning a Post *(Staff only)*

Pinned posts appear at the top of the feed above all other posts.

1. Check the **Pin this post** checkbox (only visible to staff members).
2. Set a **Pin expires on** date/time — this is required when pinning.
3. Once the expiry date passes, the pin icon is automatically hidden and the post returns to its normal position in the feed.

## Liking a Post

Click the heart icon at the bottom of any post to like it. Click again to unlike. The like count updates immediately.

*(screenshot: Like button on a post card)*

---

## Editing a Post

You can only edit posts you authored.

1. Click the **⋯** (more) menu on your post.
2. Select **Edit**.
3. Update the fields in the form and click **Save**.

*(screenshot: Post card with ⋯ menu open showing Edit option)*

---

## Deleting a Post

You can only delete posts you authored.

1. Click the **⋯** (more) menu on your post.
2. Select **Delete**.
3. Confirm the deletion in the dialog.

*(screenshot: Delete confirmation dialog)*

---

## Long Posts

If a post is longer than 4 lines, it will be truncated automatically. Click **show more** to expand the full content, and **show less** to collapse it again.

*(screenshot: Truncated post with "show more" link)*

---

## Loading More Posts

The feed loads 10 posts at a time. Click **Load more** at the bottom of the feed to fetch the next page.

*(screenshot: Load more button at bottom of feed)*
